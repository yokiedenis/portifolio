how to present simple projects for interviews

Briefly explain your project, less is better
  1. what's the project about(define Project)
  2. what tech stack you have used
  3. static or dynamic i.e reponsive, i used media queries with flex or grid
  4. Why have you used a certain library i.e bootstrap
  5. what problems did you face while building the project i.e popper CDN had an error in it, and bootstrap predefined nav bar didn't work for me
NB: if your unsure, don't give a random answer, say that you have to do some research on it or something about it
